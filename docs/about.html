About Sidestream

Sidestream is a non-commercial, single-developer passion project.

This project is motivated by a desire to bring a little more of the value, excitement, and pleasure of human conversation to our chats with AI.

Open Source

Source code is available on GitHub at github.com/ericbrandon/sidestream.

Privacy

API keys entered in the app are stored encrypted on your computer and only shared with the AI companies whose models you're using. The developer of this app has no access to your API keys.

The developer of this app also has no access to any other information about you, your chats, or anything else.
