Sidestream

A chat app with a side of insights

A free, open-source, cross-platform, cross-model AI chat app that can add useful, interesting, amusing, and critical information you wouldn't have known about otherwise.

Download

Human conversation is usually more rewarding than talking with an AI model - because most people aren't responding to what you said as a "helpful assistant".

They realize they might know something you wouldn't even ask about, and eagerly tell you about it.

They're reminded of something interesting or amusing, and steer the conversation in a more exciting direction.

They might realize that one of you misspoke, misremembered, made an incorrect assumption, or that you've been talking past each other - and they course correct the conversation.

This app does ALL of that and makes your conversations with AI way more useful and interesting than you've ever experienced before.

What are "Discoveries"?

The discovery engine specifically looks for:

  • Lesser-known but highly relevant information
  • Content you wouldn't have asked for because you didn't know it existed
  • Fascinating observations to steer the conversation in more exciting directions
  • Lateral thinking to feed your understanding
  • Unique perspectives & unconventional sources
  • Constrasting and alternative views

NOT the obvious first-page Google results, Wikipedia summaries, or mainstream articles

This app requires you to have an API key from Anthropic, OpenAI, and/or Google Gemini
